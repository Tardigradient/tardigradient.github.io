Who We Are

Pirate Club is a student organization at Utah State University dedicated to adventure, creativity, and community.

We welcome all students who want to be part of something unique and exciting!

FAQ

Do I need pirate experience?

Absolutely not! All skill levels welcome, from landlubbers to seasoned sailors.

What should I bring to meetings?

Just yourself and a sense of adventure! We provide everything else.

How do I stay updated?

Join our GroupMe to receive announcements about meetings, events, and treasure hunt updates!

Treasure Hunt Details

How It Works:

  1. Register and pay the $4 entry fee
  2. Receive your treasure map and initial clues
  3. Solve riddles and follow the trail across campus
  4. Find the hidden treasure chest
  5. Contact us to unlock the chest and claim your prize!

Prize includes: Gift cards and items from Chick-fil-A, Angie's, Raising Cane's, and more!
