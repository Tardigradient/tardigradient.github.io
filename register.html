How It Works

Start by submitting your donation of $4 to @eliglende on Venmo, and include your A number in the note. Then fill out this form to get registered, and we will provide you with a treasure map so you can get started!

Hunt until you solve the riddles and discover the chest. If you find the treasure, give us a shout and we will unlock the chest for you.


FAQ

What do I do in this treasure hunt? Use the map and the riddle that you receive to lead you along a path of clues that will help you find the hidden treasure chest! Feel free to work with a friend to crack the code. Most of the places you need to go are outdoors, so dress warm!

Where might the treasure be hidden? The treasure is hidden somewhere on the USU campus. You will not need to leave campus at any time in order to find it.

What is the treasure? The contents of the chest are a secret, but they are worth more than a meal at Chick-Fil-A and they are worth a bit of effort!

When does the treasure hunt take place? The treasure hunt will officially begin on March 1st, 2026. Registration will be open January 5th and will remain open for the duration of the treasure hunt, which will of course end when somebody discovers the location of the chest.

Can I start hunting right now? We will not put out the treasure chest or any of the clues until the hunt officially begins on March 1, but perhaps you could ponder upon the initial riddle until then.
