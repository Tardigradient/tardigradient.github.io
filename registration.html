Rules

  • Please do not share the map or clues with others. You are free to work with your friends but we kindly ask that each participant submits the $4 entry amount.
  • Don't move or tamper with any clues, and make sure you put everything back just how you found it so everyone can have a fair chance!
  • Take a photo at each location so we can confirm that you reached the chest legitimately.
  • Do not try to open or take the chest. If you find it, arrange a time with us to unlock it.
  • Keep in mind that this is a competition. Only the first group to find the chest will receive the prize!
